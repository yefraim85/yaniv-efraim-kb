Bio Family

I'm Yaniv Efraim (יניב אפרים). I describe myself as loving, content, and passionate about exploring the world, the truth, and meeting people. I'm married to Neta Frank, and we have three children — Ariel (born 2016), Noam (born 2018), and Hili (born 2021). I'm the son of Isaac and Sarah.

Worldview

I believe humans already exist in their desired spiritual state — the work of personal development is building on knowledge, presence, and meaningful existence, not chasing some far-off version of ourselves. I hold optimistic views about human nature and about our capacity for renewal.

Ideology, in short: existence as purpose, free choice, absolute truth, and a relative morality that's judged against clearly defined objectives. More on this in Worldview & Philosophy.

Background

My background spans entrepreneurship and education. I co-founded a kindergarten association and built SUMIT, a business management system now serving tens of thousands of clients. I'm currently developing SOL — a modern ashram and retreat center for long-term stays.

How I teach

My approach centers on unconditional acceptance and compassion. I work with groups through lectures, workshops, and mentoring, and I believe people already carry their own inner light — the work is self-discovery, not installation. See Lectures & Workshops for what I currently offer.

What I'm writing

Five books are in progress, including one built around the concepts that support personal growth, and another on parenting guidance. See Book of Life and On Parenting.
