Accounting for Representatives

Last updated 5 July 2026
System Finance

An accounting system built specifically for financial representatives — a narrower, purpose-built counterpart to SUMIT.

Why a separate system

Financial representatives have accounting needs — commission tracking, client-facing statements, compliance record-keeping — that a general business platform handles awkwardly at best. This system exists to serve that niche directly rather than bolting it onto something broader.
