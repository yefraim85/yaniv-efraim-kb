Platform Entrepreneurship

SUMIT is a business management platform I built — invoicing, payments, and the day-to-day operational backbone for a business. It now serves tens of thousands of clients.

Origin

SUMIT grew out of the same entrepreneurial thread as co-founding a kindergarten association: seeing an operational gap firsthand and building the system that should have already existed to close it.

Note: the same instinct — build the missing infrastructure rather than work around it — shows up again in SOL.

What it does

  • Invoicing and payment collection for small and mid-sized businesses.
  • Operational tooling so the business owner spends less time on admin.
  • Scaled to tens of thousands of clients.
