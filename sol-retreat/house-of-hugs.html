Last updated 5 July 2026
Education Kindergarten

Democratic education kindergartens, grown out of the same association I co-founded early in my entrepreneurial background. "House of Hugs" is the philosophy in miniature: children as capable participants in their own environment, not just recipients of instruction.

Democratic education, briefly

Children have real say in the rules and rhythms of their own space. It's the same underlying belief that shows up across this whole knowledge base — that people (including very young ones) already carry what they need, and the environment's job is to make room for it rather than override it.

Note: see Worldview & Philosophy for the belief this is built on, and Parenting for how it plays out at home.
