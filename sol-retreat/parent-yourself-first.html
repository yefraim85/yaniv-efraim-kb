Parent Yourself First

Last updated 5 July 2026
Community Copenhagen

A community built on a simple premise: you parent your children more effectively once you've done some parenting of yourself first. It runs as an active WhatsApp group, plus in-person parents' meetings in Copenhagen.

How it connects to the parenting work

This is the community layer around the ideas in Parenting — a place to talk through the harder, more personal side of raising children, rather than just reading about it.

Format: ongoing WhatsApp conversation for day-to-day support, with periodic in-person meetings in Copenhagen for deeper sessions.
