Retreat Thailand

A wellness retreat in Koh Phangan, Thailand, that I facilitate — and the direct predecessor to SOL, the longer-term retreat center I'm now building.

What it is

A space for people to step out of ordinary routines and spend concentrated time on the things this whole knowledge base is about: presence, honesty, and connection. It's shorter and more contained than SOL will be, which makes it a useful proving ground for what actually works.

Relationship to SOL: School of Life answers "does this work for a week?" — SOL is the answer to "what does this look like as a place people can live for months?"

Facilitation style

Same approach as everywhere else I teach: unconditional acceptance and compassion, delivered through lectures, workshops, and mentoring. See Lectures & Workshops for the general format.
