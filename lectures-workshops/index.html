Lectures & Workshops

Last updated 5 July 2026
Offerings Hebrew / English

I work with groups through lectures, workshops, and mentoring, in Hebrew or English, online. The approach is the same across formats: unconditional acceptance and compassion, on the belief that people already carry their own inner light and mainly need the conditions for self-discovery.

Lectures

Hour-long online lectures covering topics like self-management, parenting, and personal development. Built to give a group a clear, usable framework in a single session — grounded in the ideas in Worldview & Philosophy.

Workshops

90-minute workshops addressing parenting, communication, and self-care. More interactive than the lectures — built around practice, not just explanation.

Format: lectures ≈ 60 minutes · workshops ≈ 90 minutes · both run online, in Hebrew or English.

Where this connects

  • Topics draw directly from Personal Development and Parenting.
  • The in-person version of this work happens at School of Life and, longer-term, at SOL.
