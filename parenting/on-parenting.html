A comprehensive guide for parents — the attempt to put my whole approach to raising children into one place, rather than scattered across tips and conversations.

Starting point

Same as everywhere else in this knowledge base: children already carry what they need. Parenting isn't installing values from scratch — it's protecting the conditions where a child's own curiosity, confidence, and connection can develop without being talked out of them. See Worldview & Philosophy for the underlying framework.

What the guide will cover

  • Unconditional acceptance as the baseline stance, not just a technique for hard moments.
  • Communication that stays honest without becoming harsh.
  • How much structure vs. freedom, and how that answer changes with age.
  • Repairing after conflict — with the child, and with yourself.
Note: this guide and Parenting Tips are meant to work together — the guide is the "why," the tips are the "what to do at 7pm when everyone's tired."
