Notes Practical

Short, practical notes — the things I actually reach for in the moment, as opposed to the fuller reasoning in On Parenting.

Everyday tips

  • Name the feeling before addressing the behavior — connection first, correction second.
  • Offer real choices, not fake ones — kids notice the difference immediately.
  • Repair out loud. Let them see you notice when you got it wrong.
  • Protect unstructured time — it's not wasted time, it's where a lot of the development happens.
Note: this list grows as I notice what actually works with Ariel, Noam, and Hili — it's a living page, not a finished list.

When it's hard

The tip that matters most on the hard days: unconditional acceptance doesn't mean no boundaries — it means the boundary and the love aren't in competition.
