Book Identity

A book of questions meant to be explored together as a family — a way into Jewish identity and tradition that starts with curiosity rather than instruction.

Why questions, not answers

Consistent with the rest of my approach to parenting: the goal isn't handing children a fixed set of beliefs, it's giving them good questions to sit with, at an age where sitting with a question is still genuinely interesting rather than a chore.

Note: this pairs naturally with On Parenting — same philosophy, applied specifically to identity and tradition.
