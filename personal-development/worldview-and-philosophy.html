Philosophy Foundational

This is the worldview underneath everything else in this knowledge base — the lens I use for parenting, teaching, building SUMIT, and designing SOL. It's not a finished system; it's the current, working version.

The core belief

Humans already exist in their desired spiritual state. Personal development isn't a project of becoming someone else — it's built on knowledge, presence, and meaningful existence. The work is uncovering what's already there, not installing something new.

Practical read: if growth is uncovering rather than installing, then the most useful thing I can offer someone — in a lecture, a workshop, or as a parent — is unconditional acceptance, not a curriculum.

The ideological framework

  • Existence as purpose — being here, fully, is itself the point.
  • Free choice — people are agents, not products of circumstance.
  • Absolute truth — some things are true independent of opinion, and it's worth pursuing them rather than settling for comfortable ambiguity.
  • Relative morality — judged against clearly defined objectives, not fixed rules applied without context.

Optimism about human nature

I hold an optimistic view of people and of our collective capacity for renewal. This shows up directly in how I run groups: I assume people carry their own inner light and only need the conditions to discover it — see Lectures & Workshops for how that plays out in practice.

Loving, content, and passionate about exploring the world, the truth, and meeting people.
